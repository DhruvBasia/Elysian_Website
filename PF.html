About Us

Elysian 2025 is the flagship cultural & technical festival of University School of Automation & Robotics (USAR), GGSIPU, taking place from 15-17 October 2025 at East Delhi Campus, GGSIPU. This three-day celebration transforms the campus into a hub of innovation, creativity, and collaboration. Technical highlights include hackathons, coding battles, ideathons, AR/VR showcases, robotics competitions, hardware hackathons, Shark Tank-style pitches, e-sports tournaments, quizzes, and capture-the-flag challenges. Cultural evenings feature DJ Evening, Garba Night, Music and Dance Performances, workshops, student-led stalls, and fun activities, making Elysian a perfect blend of technology and entertainment.
